Classic Chicken Soup

Soo Delicious

Description

Chicken soup is a classic and comforting dish that's perfect for a cold day or when you're feeling under the weather. To make this recipe, start by boiling a whole chicken in water with chopped onions, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaf, salt, and black pepper. Cook the soup for about an hour until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool. Then, remove the skin and bones and shred the meat. If you want to add rice or egg noodles, cook them in the broth until tender, and then add the shredded chicken and chopped parsley to the pot. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed and serve hot with some crusty bread on the side.

his recipe makes a large pot of soup that serves 6-8 people. It's a nourishing and satisfying meal that's easy to make and can be customized to your liking. You can add different vegetables, spices, or herbs to the broth or use a different type of pasta or grain. It's a versatile recipe that's sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, about 4 pounds
  • 4 quarts water
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 4 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  • Rinse the chicken and remove the giblets. Place the chicken in a large pot and cover with 4 quarts of water.
  • Add the chopped onions, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaf, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Bring the water to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  • Cook the soup for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  • Remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool. Then, remove the skin and bones and shred the meat.
  • If using rice or egg noodles, add them to the pot and cook until tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  • Add the shredded chicken and chopped parsley to the pot and stir to combine. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  • Serve hot with some crusty bread on the side.
