Spaghetti Recipe

Soo Delicious

Description

Spaghetti is a classic and delicious pasta dish that's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. To make this recipe, start by boiling a pound of spaghetti in salted water until it's al dente. While the pasta is cooking, make the sauce by saut√©ing chopped onion and garlic in olive oil until the onion is soft and translucent. Then add a can of crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes to the skillet and simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Finally, add chopped basil and reserved pasta water to the sauce and simmer for another 5 minutes before adding the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and tossing everything together. Top with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and serve hot.

This recipe is a great base for a variety of spaghetti dishes. You can add cooked meat, vegetables, or herbs to the sauce to make it your own. This recipe serves 4-6 people and takes about 30 minutes to make. It's a simple and delicious way to enjoy one of the most popular pasta dishes in the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add 1 tablespoon of salt and the spaghetti to the pot and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain the spaghetti and reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, sugar, 1 teaspoon of salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes to the skillet. Simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the reserved pasta water and chopped basil to the sauce and stir to combine. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to coat the pasta in the sauce. Serve hot with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
