Delicious Rice Recipe

Soo Delicious

Description

To make this delicious rice recipe, you'll need just a few simple ingredients: long-grain white rice, water, butter or oil, and salt. Start by rinsing the rice in a fine-mesh strainer under cold running water for a minute or two, until the water runs clear. This step helps to remove excess starch from the rice and ensures that it cooks evenly. In a medium saucepan, bring the water, butter or oil, and salt to a boil over high heat. Once the mixture comes to a boil, add the rinsed rice and stir it once to ensure it's evenly distributed. Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid, and simmer the rice for 18 minutes.

After 18 minutes, remove the saucepan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. This allows the rice to absorb any remaining liquid and ensures that it's fluffy and tender. Finally, fluff the rice with a fork and serve. You can also add other ingredients like garlic or onion to the recipe while cooking to add more flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs clear.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water, butter or oil, and salt to a boil over high heat.
  3. Add the rice to the saucepan and stir once.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid, and simmer for 18 minutes.
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
